## Selected Approach: Idea 1 - The "Blueprint & Architect" Aesthetic

**Reasoning:**
The user's LinkedIn headline emphasizes "Engineering Teams", "Systems", "Architecture", and "AI-Accelerated". The "Blueprint & Architect" aesthetic perfectly aligns with the persona of a **Fractional CTO** who comes in to fix broken processes and build reliable systems. It communicates technical competence, structure, and a "hands-on" approach which is crucial for early-stage founders who need someone to actually *build* and *ship*, not just advise. It differentiates from generic SaaS marketing sites (Idea 2) and purely management consultancies (Idea 3).

**Design Philosophy:**
"Structured Precision." We will use a design that feels like a well-architected system itself.

**Implementation Details:**
-   **Font:** `Space Grotesk` (Headings) + `Inter` (Body).
-   **Colors:** Dark mode base (Slate-950), with "Blueprint Blue" (Blue-500) and "Success Green" (Emerald-400) accents.
-   **Visuals:** Abstract geometric patterns representing distributed systems and network nodes.
